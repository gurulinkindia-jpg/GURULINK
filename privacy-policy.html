GURULINK is committed to protecting the privacy of students, parents, teachers, tutors, coaches, skill trainers, and educational institutions using our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect user information.

We may collect the following information from users:

  • Name

  • Phone number
  • Profile information
  • Educational and professional details
  • Teacher, tutor, coach, or institute details

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts

  • Provide customer support and important updates

GURULINK does not sell personal information. User information may be shared only in the following cases:

  • With other users where necessary for educational connection and profile visibility

  • With payment, hosting, database, or communication service providers
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process

Teachers, tutors, coaches, trainers, and institutes may create public profiles on GURULINK. Information such as name, photo, qualification, experience, subjects, services, fees, address, contact details, and uploaded profile content may be visible to students, parents, and other users.
